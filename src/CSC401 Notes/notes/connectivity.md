# connectivity
##
- A graph is connected when $\exists$ a [[path]] between all vertices.
- Two points are connected when $\exists$ a path between them.
- $\forall$ [[path complete]] $\implies$ [[connectivity]]
- The opposite is not true 
- G is connected when $\forall u, v \in V$, u and v are connected
