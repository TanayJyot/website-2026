


## Product of Sets
If A and B are sets. Define: $$ A \times B = \{(x,y): x \in A \land y \in B \} $$
This is called the [[cartesian product]].
Basically the order of the elements matter. 
Example:
$$ (1,\pi) \in \mathbb{Z} \times \Re $$
$$ (\pi, 1) \notin \mathbb{Z} \times \Re $$
The first coordinate belongs to the first set and the second coordinate belongs to the second set.
