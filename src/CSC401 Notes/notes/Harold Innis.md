# Harold Innis
##
- He talked about how empires that extracted wealth were also affected by the wealth because it made them feel superior and created a sense of their right to impose themselves.
- He felt that people that studied/were in the centre of empires were blinded by their own wealth, success, myths and power to what was truly happening to themselves as to those they ruled.
- He thought that his work became useless as world war 2 came along and started looking into what he may have done wrong. 
- He thought media might explain where we went off track and explain how we might get back in the right direction. 
- What makes him impressive is going on the opposite side of what he had been doing his entire life. 

> The formal essay was invented by an early Chinese dynasty to prevent intellectuals from thinking too much.
