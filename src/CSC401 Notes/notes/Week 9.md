### Redefining work and economy
- producers and consumers are turning into the same people. 
	- When you ‘mod’ a game you kinda act as the producer making changes to the game. 
- Content creation as work is done intrinsic satisfaction than just for compensation. 
- Creating content may be the motivation for their work. 
- working without pay has become more common, which would have seemed bad before the internet.

### Fairness and payment
- first motive is to express themselves creatively and the last is money. 
- people generating the content get nothing while the site gets money. 
- wealth accrues mostly to the owner of the platform.
	- Capitalism stuff where people at the top earn the most money. 
- although productive work can be intrinsically rewarding, there is a fairness of payment question.
	- 30% charge on in game items for example.
