# statistical language modelling
##
- you can use [[Conditional PMF]] to chain [[n-grams]] and compare the probability of different sequences of n-grams

$$P(A|B, C) > P(B|A, C)$$
- then you can use the [[chain rule for probability]] to do this for larger sequences
