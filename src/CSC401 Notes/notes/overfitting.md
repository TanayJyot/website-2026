# overfitting
## The Problem with Overfitting
- When a data is overfitting then that means that it would recognise the data perfectly in the real world
- The problem with this is that our training data contained noise and this noise would also be considered as ‘fit’ during overfitting even though it is not a part of the real world
- This is what would lead to errors.
