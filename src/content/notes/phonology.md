### ambiguity

- there are different things a word could mean based ont he way it is said so this becomes a problem for example. “That is an object” and I object
- for [[speech recognition]] this would mean things like too much and two items
- given context you might be able to resolve these but not always
- [[syntax]] also allows you to resolve this ambiguity since you can use your grammatical knowledge to “make sense” of what is being said
