### Geometric Meaning
![[Pictures/Pasted image 20240522193115.png]]

$$v\cdot w = |v||w|\cos{\theta}$$
- This makes the [[Cauchy-Swartz Inequality]] trivial because $-1\le  \cos\theta \le 1$  

#### Statements 
Assuming $v, w \neq 0$ 
- they are in the same direction  $\iff v \cdot w = |v||w|$
- they are in the opposite direction $\iff v \cdot w = -|v||w|$ 
- they are perpendicular $\iff v \cdot w = 0$
