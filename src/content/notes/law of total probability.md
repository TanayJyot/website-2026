# law of total probability
##
- You take events $C_n$ s.t. 
- $C_1 \cap C_2 ... = \emptyset$ 
- $C_1 \cup C_2 ... = \Omega$ 
- Then any new event $A$ would be the sum of the probability of all $C$S

$$P(A) = P(A \cap C_1) + P(A \cap C_2)...$$
$$P(A|C_1) \times P(C_1) + P(A|C_2) \times P(C_2)...$$
