# interneuron
##
 Neurons that have neither sensory nor motor functions are called interneurons. _Inter_ in this case means “between,” because many interneurons form bridges between sensory and motor neurons. The knee-jerk reflex forms a simple arc between a sensory neuron and a motor neuron and does not require interneurons. However, interneurons play important roles in other reflexes and throughout the nervous system.
