Author: [[Fyodor Mikhailovich Dostoyevsky]]
# The Book in 3 Sentences
1. Having an idea can change the entire course of your plans and make everything else absurd. 
2. As a person you need to believe in something greater than yourself.
3. You don’t become an honourable person for the world, that’s why it doesn’t matter whether the world remembers your honour or not you do it for your own sake. 


# Impressions
[[Fyodor Mikhailovich Dostoyevsky]] is an amazing writer. The way he describes things is as if everything is real even in a work of fiction. It’s very similar to reality and the way things happen in it. Also there is everything, the book can interest a general reader and someone who is willing to learn out of it. You are the ones who decides what you become and you decide to have your interest.

# Who should read it?
Anyone who is into fiction and is willing to go in depth while still retaining life lessons. It’s an amazing way in which he teaches things. The book is long though and it isn’t as easy read, so someone needs to be devoted enough to read it.
