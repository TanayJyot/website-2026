# sensory neuron
##
- Send information from senses to nervous system. 
-  Sensory neurons carry information from the external environment or from the body back to the CNS. In the knee-jerk reflex, sensory neurons tell the spinal cord that a muscle has been stretched by the tap of the hammer.
