# motor neuron
##
- Used to send signal from the [[central nervous system|CNS]] to the muscles so that they do something.
- Motor neurons carry commands from the CNS back to the muscles and glands of the body. In response to information about the stretched muscle, the spinal cord sends a message through motor neurons back to your leg, telling the muscle to contract to counteract the stretch. You know what happens next—your foot kicks as the muscles contract.
