# conflict
##
- occurs when there are 2 versions of the same file and the top contains different set of stuff written on the same line.

___
## [[team]]

> occurs when one person’s or one group’s needs do not match those of another and attempts may be made to block the opposing side’s intentions or goals. 

- caused by disagreement over goals or priorities. 
- also caused by task related issues, incompatible personalities, being overtires and overstressed and team diversity. 
- Diversity awareness training programs can help
- conflict itself isn’t the problem but what kinda conflict you have matters.
	- [[cognitive conflict]]
	- [[affective conflict]]
- leaders have to encourage good communication and set up an environment that makes it possible to do so.
- look at situations and conditions that might be causing conflict. 
- teal building, listening, role playing exercises.
